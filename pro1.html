Tank Shooter

INTRODUCTION
Graphics provides one of the most natural means of communicating with a computer, since our highly developed 2D and 3D pattern-recognition abilities allow us to perceive and process pictorial data rapidly and efficiently. Interactive computer graphics is the most important means of producing pictures since the invention of photography and television. It has the added advantage that, with the computer, we can make pictures not only of concrete real world objects but also of abstract, synthetic objects, such as mathematical surfaces and of data that have no inherent geometry, such as survey results. This mini-project demonstrates a simple game of TankShooter. TankShooter is a single user video game. The objective of this game is to shoot the opponent tank coming infront of green tank. The game is played by destroying the enemy tanks by shooting it or simply dodging it. Shooting the enemy tanks will give you 20 points whereas dodging it will give you 10 points.
